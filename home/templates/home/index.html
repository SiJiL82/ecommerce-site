About Mini Sew N Sew

Hello! Welcome to my website, I am so delighted that you are visiting Mini Sew N Sew! My name is Jeri and I am mum to two gorgeous girls, who are the main inspiration for my venture. I love making their clothes and after a few years of doing so, I finally decided it was time to take the leap into making these creations available to the world. Come take a walk with me into my happy place, this site is full of my creations, all made to order from my home in Wirral and made with love and joy. I pride myself in attention to detail, and creating things designed to last the test of time. Please get in touch with any questions, custom requests and just for a chat too, cannot wait to hear from you.

Our Team

Jerilyn Brown

Founder and Maker

Hello! This is me, probably the only photo you will get from me, as I leave the modelling to the little ones! I am the creator and I spend most of my time at my trusty work horses (the machine and overlocker), I play many roles, but my favourite is custom selecting the most exciting fabrics I can get my hands on and creating unique sewing patterns for one of a kind clothing. I'm a one-woman band - I package and post, photograph and market, I'm head of accounting and those customer relations questions all come to me too!

Kitty

Postal Assistant and Head Model

Since joining Mini Sew N Sew, my first daughter Kitty has played a crucial role in the continued success of the company! She is where it all started, the first recipient of Mini Sew N Sew clothes, the sassiest model, and the biggest fan - I am a bit biased obviously!

Millie

Also known as 'the Meme', this gorgeous team member is an absolute dream to make clothes for and wears almost anything I pop her in. The giggliest member of the team, and the one who makes the days when post office runs are not as smooth as I'd like a lot more joyful to manage.
